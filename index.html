Privacy Policy for Binary Calculator

Introduction

Welcome to Binary Calculator, a dedicated tool for performing binary calculations. This Privacy Policy underscores our commitment to user privacy and data security. Our app is designed with the sole purpose of providing a reliable and efficient calculation service without the need to collect, store, or process personal data.

No Collection of Personal Information

Binary Calculator is uniquely designed to operate without collecting any personal information from its users. We value the privacy of our users and have developed our app to ensure that user privacy is respected and protected. As such, we do not require or store names, email addresses, location data, or any other form of personal data.

Data Processing and Storage

Since Binary Calculator does not collect personal data, there is no processing or storage of such information. All calculations are processed locally on your device. This approach ensures that your privacy is maintained, as no data leaves your device.

User Data Security

Our commitment to not collecting personal data inherently ensures the highest level of data security for our users. As there is no data collection or storage, the risks associated with data breaches, unauthorized access, or data misuse are effectively nullified.

User Rights and Consent

Given our no data collection policy, traditional concerns regarding user rights such as access, correction, deletion, or data portability are not applicable. By using Binary Calculator, users can be assured that there is no need to consent to data collection, as no personal data is involved in the use of our app.

Third-Party Services

Binary Calculator maintains its commitment to privacy by not integrating with third-party services that could collect personal data. Our app stands as a standalone tool, free from external data-sharing practices, thereby ensuring your calculations remain private.

Compliance with Laws

Binary Calculator's approach to not collecting personal data inherently complies with data protection and privacy laws globally. By avoiding data collection, we ensure our users' rights and privacy are inherently protected, in line with legal requirements.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This policy reflects our current practices and will be updated to reflect any future changes, especially in the unlikely event of a change in our data collection practices. We will provide notifications of any changes to this policy to keep our users informed.
